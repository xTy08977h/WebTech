TIPS TO GROW YOUR STUBBORN CALVES
Most people find that their calves are resistant to growth. Every time you take a step the calves are activated, and they're burdened with carrying around your bodyweight all day long. This means that you must attack the calves Opens a New Window. fiercely to make them get bigger and stronger. Conventional training just won't do when it comes to calves.

TIPS CALVES WORKOUT FOR ADVANCED LIFTERS
First of all, Stretch and strengthen your calves while also lengthening your hamstrings and lower-back muscles. Second, Stand with one foot parallel to a line in the floor, and lift the opposite foot off the ground. Then, hop over the line on one foot as fast as possible, making sure to keep your hips stable. After that, do 10 reps on one foot, then do 10 reps on the other foot. Next, set up three mini hurdles in a line, each 2 feet apart. Then, Start by straddling the first obstacle. Lastly, run laterally over the obstacles without crossing your feet. Go out and back 10 times.
